Adobo Recipe

Adobos are one of the culinary dishes in the Philippines, which a classic filipino dish filipinos love. This recipe will teach you how to cook adobo and serve it into your dinners!

This dish is made chicken and added with sauce that has a vinegar and soy taste!

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, rinsed and patted dry
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tablespoons pickling spice, wrappped in cheesecloth

Steps:

  1. Head oil in a large pan over medium heat until oil is shimmering. Cook garlic in oil for no more than 30 seconds. Add all of the chicken to the pot; cook, stirring frequently, until chicken is white all over. Do not brown.
  2. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar and water, and add the pickling spice. Make sure the spice ball is submerged. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer, and place lid on pot so that some steam can escape. Simmer for 1 hour, or until chicken is very tender.
