Adobo Recipe

Adobos are one of the culinary dishes in the Philippines, which a classic filipino dish filipinos love. This recipe will teach you how to cook adobo and serve it into your dinners!

This dish is made chicken and added with sauce that has a vinegar and soy taste!

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, rinsed and patted dry
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tablespoons pickling spice, wrappped in cheesecloth
